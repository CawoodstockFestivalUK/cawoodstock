We have secured a small amount of additional parking for Cawoodstock, but please still try to minimise cars within the village by car-sharing or using taxis/lifts/drop-offs rather than parking in the village. We cannot guarantee a parking space for everyone, and cars parked illegally or causing an obstruction will not be tolerated.
