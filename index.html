Welcome to Cawoodstock!

a brand new grassroots music, arts and cultural festival in Cawood, Yorkshire
organised by the people for the people

Friday August 24th - Sunday August 26th 2018

  • For Charities and local good causes

    Cawoodstock raises money for good causes and charities to make our local area better for everyone

  • Volunteer organised

    Organised by a small team of volunteers, and the music venues themselves, for a truly homebrew festival feel.

  • Cawood has no parking!

    Cawood is a small, ancient village with extremely limited parking. PLEASE try to use public transport, cycles, taxis, walking or any other mode of transport than car. If you must drive - car share - and park sensibly.

  • Our first year

    This is the first year of Cawoodstock, we'd love people to get involved to make it better, and also we welcome your views.
