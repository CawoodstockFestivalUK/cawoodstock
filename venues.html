Participating festival venues

  • The Castle Inn

    7 Wistowgate, Cawood, Selby YO8 3SH

  • The Jolly Sailor

    The Jolly Sailor is Cawood's regular music venue pub, with regular music acts every Friday and Saturday, a monthly acoustic jam session, and Cawood bands The Sicknotes and Chris and Sarah. Contact Kathy Reece-King via Facebook

    5 Market Place, Cawood YO8 3SR

  • The Ferry Inn

    The Ferry Inn is an historic 16th century inn, serving 5 real ales and fine pub cuisine in a traditional pub atmosphere.

    2 King St, Cawood, Selby YO8 3TL

  • White Cross Waterski Club

    The White Cross Ski Club is a water ski and wakeboard club, first opened in 1961, and with a brand new purpose built member bar and recreation facility. The Ski Club is holding a Family Fun Day over the Cawoodstock weekend, with music, food, boat rides on the river Ouse, and lots more. Information on starting water ski and wakeboarding, and Club membership, can be obtained from any existing member or by contacting the Club via their Facebook page.

    Cawoodstock @ White Cross Waterski Club are supporting: https://www.YorkshireAirAmulance.org.uk, https://www.YorkRescueBoat.com, raising funds for a defibrillator to be located at the ski club for village use, and raising money to support Cawoodstock 2019.

    Rythergate, Cawood, Selby YO8 3TP

  • Grangeside Nurseries

    Grangeside Nurseries and Cafe, a hidden gem in the heart of historic Cawood. We pride ourselves on high quality plants specialising in those that are especially useful to bees and pollinating insects and actively promote environmentally conscientious gardening. We have a cosy cafe with plenty of outdoor seating, serving breakfast baps, lunches and great coffee and cakes

    Thorpe Lane, Cawood, YO8 3SG

  • Cawood Park Caravan Site

    Ryther Rd, Cawood, York YO8 3TT

  • The Old Boys' School

    The Old Boys' School is used as a community centre and village hall for Cawood.

    Over Cawoodstock Saturday, the Old Boys' School will host Refreshments & children's activities including design-your-own Cawoodstock wristband & maracas, sand art, rock painting & much more! There will be a small charge to cover costs. All children must be accompanied by an adult.

    Donations given at the OBS activities will support Cawood Rainbows and Brownies.

    Old Boys School Lane, off Rythergate, YO8 3TY
